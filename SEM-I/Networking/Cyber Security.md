Confidentiality
- Confidentiality related with law, - legal term refers to the duty of an individual to refrain from sharing confidential info. with others without express consent of other party.
- in cyber sec. Confidentiality is all about preserving the authorized restrictions on information access and disclosure, including means for protecting personal privacy.
- Also means controlling access to files either in a storage or in transit.
- Ex. Data encryption - 2 way factor authentication.

Integrity
- these measures includes file permissions and user access controls.
- ensuring the integrity is a matter of - version control - meaning - making sure that only right people can change the document(s).
- Also requires audit trail of changes and a fallback position in case change prove detrimental.

Availability
- Cinderella of the information security as it is rarely discussed.
- Availability means information should be consistently and readily accessible for authorized parties. This involves properly maintaining the hardware and technical infrastructure and systems that hold and display the information.
